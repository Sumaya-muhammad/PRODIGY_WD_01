Know About Us!

Welcome to Faraya Accessories, where we turn your favorite moments into timeless keepsakes! Established in 2022, Faraya Accessories started as a small Instagram business, offering personalized vintage filmstrip keychains that capture your most cherished memories.

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a unique way for people to carry their memories with them. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a full-fledged e-commerce platform, dedicated to rekindling the magic of capturing and preserving special moments in an innovative way.

Faraya Accessories is more than just a business—it’s a passion project born out of a love for photography, memories, and creativity. What started as a hobby has grown into a meaningful way to connect with customers around the world, helping them preserve their precious moments. Our aim is to give you something special—because every memory deserves to be celebrated.

Our Product: Vintage Filmstrip Keychains

At Faraya Accessories, we specialize in custom filmstrip keychains, where you can showcase 5, 7, or 10 of your favorite photos in a stylish, portable way. Whether it’s a gift for someone special or a keepsake for yourself, these keychains are more than just accessories—they’re a journey through your most treasured moments.
